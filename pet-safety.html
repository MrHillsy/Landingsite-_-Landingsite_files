Pet Safety Tips

Keep your furry friends safe, healthy, and happy

1. Quick Tips for All Pet Owners

  • Keep emergency numbers (vet, poison control) handy.
  • Microchip your pet and keep contact info up to date.
  • Supervise pets around children and new visitors.
  • Train basic commands for safety and recall.
  • Stay informed about local hazards (wildlife, weather, etc.).

2. Vet Visits & Preventive Care

  • Regular vet visits and vaccinations.
  • Use flea and tick preventatives.
  • Monitor changes in health or behavior.

3. Resources & Further Reading

  • ASPCA: General Pet Safety Tips
  • AVMA: Pet Safety Resources
  • Humane Society: Pet Safety Tips

For more information, consult your veterinarian or local animal welfare organization.

4. Outdoor Safety

  • Always use a leash or secure fencing.
  • Provide shade and water on hot days.
  • Be mindful of toxic plants and dangerous wildlife.

5. Seasonal Safety

  • Protect pets from extreme heat or cold.
  • Keep antifreeze and de-icing salts away from pets.
  • Watch for signs of heatstroke or frostbite.
